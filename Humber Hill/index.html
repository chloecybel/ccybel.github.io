"THE HUMBER HILL HOUSE"

The design of the Humber Hill House was inspired by the site location, Humber Bay Arch Bridge, and also considered my habits. The initial idea was to integrate the house into the hills of Humber Bay to unite with the ambience of the serene site. This house aims to integrate with nature. The large windows allow a relationship with the outdoor elements and keeping a simple interior create a focus on the natural surrounding elements.

The geometry of the building create an organized pattern through a rectangular structure, but the different elongations and heights can be perceived as disorganized which is apart of my habits and became a focused concept of my design. The pathway between three different rooms connected by stairs was inspired by a musical staff. Although the shape stay harmonious, there is dissonance within the height and length of each rectangular structure.
